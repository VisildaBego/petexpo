About the Pet Expo

Enjoy a unique shopping experience for both you and your pet! Pet Expo is the largest full-line pet store with over 35,000 square feet and more than 20,000 different items. We feature Dog & Cat, Small Animal, Tame Bird, Aquatic, and Wild Bird departments! We also have a grooming salon (Vanity Fur) on site. In addition, we are stocked with gifts you cannot resist! Each department is fully staffed with friendly pet specialists to answer all your pet care needs!

Our likable staff is encouraged to appreciate every furry friend (and their owner) on an individual basis, knowing many of them by name! At Pet Expo, we pride ourselves on loyal customer service. Our knowledgeable staff is kept current with the latest industry trends and is eager to answer any pet related question. Pet Expo has the largest variety of pet food and supplies and will always strive for the lowest possible price for our customer!
